In today's digital age, small and mid-sized businesses (SMBs), local school districts and municipalities face significant cybersecurity threats.

According to the 2024 Verizon Data Breach Investigations Report, these organizations are prime targets for cyberattacks due to their often-limited security resources. Common issues include data breaches, malware attacks, and phishing schemes, which can result in severe financial losses, operational disruptions, and reputational damage.

The impact of a cyber incident can be devastating.

For SMBs, a single breach can compromise sensitive customer data, leading to loss of trust and potential legal ramifications. Local school districts, on the other hand, risk exposing student information, disrupting educational operations, and facing increased recovery costs. Many of these organizations lack the expertise and resources to effectively combat evolving cyber threats, making them vulnerable to increasingly sophisticated attacks.
